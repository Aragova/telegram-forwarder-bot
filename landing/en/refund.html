Refund Policy

Refund terms for using the ViMi Bot SaaS service.

ViMi Bot · January 1, 2026
1. General provisions 2. When refunds are possible 3. When refunds are not granted 4. SaaS specifics 5. Abuse prevention 6. How to request a refund

1. General provisions

ViMi Bot is a digital SaaS service provided remotely. The service is considered provided from the moment access to the functionality is granted.

2. When a refund may be possible

  • the Service cannot be used due to the Provider’s fault;
  • a critical error fully blocks use of the paid functionality;
  • duplicate or erroneous payment occurred.

3. When refunds are not provided

  • the user changed their mind about using the Service;
  • the user did not understand the functionality;
  • Telegram restricted or blocked the account;
  • the Service has already been used;
  • the Terms of Service were violated.

4. SaaS service specifics

Under Article 781 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, the service is considered provided after access is granted. Refund after the start of service use is not guaranteed.

5. Abuse prevention

The Provider may refuse a refund in case of signs of abuse of rights under Article 10 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, attempted fraud, or use of the Service followed by a refund demand.

6. Request procedure

To request a refund, contact support:

  • Telegram: @vimi_support_bot
  • Email: vimi.support@gmail.com

Review period: up to 10 business days.
